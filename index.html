About Me

Hi, I'm Jose Aguila, a scientist with a passion for leveraging scientific machine learning to tackle engineering challenges in fluid dynamics and naval engineering.

Research Highlights

Current research focuses on boundary layer control, non-linear ship motions, and integrating machine learning with physical laws for innovative solutions.

Projects

Boundary Layer Control

Innovative techniques to optimize flow over marine vessels for improved performance.

Vortex Induced Vibrations

Predicting vortex induced vibrations in Steel Catenary Risers (SCR)

Physics-Informed ML

Blending machine learning models with physical laws to enhance predictive accuracy.

Machine Learning of Non-linear Seakeeping

Predicting ship motions in severe sea states with non-linear phenomena such as parametric rolling.
