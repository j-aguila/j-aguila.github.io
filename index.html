About Me

Hi, I'm Jose del Aguila Ferrandis, a scientist with a passion for leveraging scientific machine learning to tackle engineering challenges in fluid dynamics and naval engineering.

Research Highlights

Current research focuses on boundary layer control, non-linear ship motions, and integrating machine learning with physical laws for innovative engineering solutions.
